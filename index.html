The Badrudhuja Islamic Center was established in Kuttalur a few years ago under the leadership of Syed Shihabuddin Bukhari Kadalundi. Badruduja has been making great strides in the field of intellectual service in the past. The revolutionary touches that marked the institution in Karma Gotha in a short period of time are invaluable. Badr Duja has already gifted to the community hundreds of eminent scholars who have made a permanent mark on the canvas of time. Badr Duja seeks to preserve the traditional Dars and recognize the need of the time for religious-physical integration. In addition to religious studies, higher studies in the field of physics are also made available to students under one umbrella. In addition to the dissemination of knowledge, the institution has a number of initiatives for the spiritual upliftment of the general public. The weekly Badr Moulid Sadass and the Mazanta 'Tajeelul Futuh Majlis are the most memorable steps of Badr Duja in the spiritual realm. The Badr Moulid audience will witness the Karimpili Badr Masjid on Fridays after the Subhi prayer. Thousands of people lined up for the fulfillment of their desires and the fulfillment of their purpose. Notable among them is Tajeel al-Futuh Majis, written by the famous Sufi saint Marakarutty Musliar, who died in 1358 AH, in order to get the desired results very quickly. Many believers also congregate at the monthly Majlis. Hope everybody on this site also had a great day!
